Finance Tracker Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-02-23 | Last Updated: 2026-02-23

This Privacy Policy explains how Finance Tracker (the "App") handles your personal information and financial records. We value your privacy and data protection.

1. Information We Process

  • Financial records: income, expenses, categories, notes, dates, and other data you choose to enter.
  • Security settings: local settings such as PIN and biometric unlock preferences.

2. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored locally on your device by default.
The developer does not collect, upload, or retain your bookkeeping data by default.

3. Purpose of Data Use

Your data is used only to provide core app functionality, including bookkeeping, statistics, and local security features. Your data is not used for advertising profiling.

4. Data Sharing

In default local mode, the App does not share your financial data with third parties. If a future version introduces optional cloud sync and you choose to enable it, additional disclosures and consent steps will be provided.

5. Data Security

  • You may enable PIN and biometric unlock to strengthen local access control.
  • You are responsible for keeping your device and access credentials secure.

6. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You may edit or delete records locally at any time. Uninstalling the App or clearing app data may permanently remove local data.

7. Liability Boundary

To the extent permitted by law, the developer is not responsible for local data loss or exposure caused by device damage, system issues, user actions, malware, or unauthorized third-party access.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to product or legal changes. Updated versions will be published on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the project release page or available in-app contact channels.
